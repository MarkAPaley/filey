Privacy Policy

filey (this “App”) does not transmit information to anyone, other than making requests for web pages from www.epguides.com .

It does not even store or print your name.

The App reads a text file and web pages from www.epguides.com , for TV series that you select, based upon names of files that you drop onto the App.

The standard Lazarus TIpHtmlPanelBrowser component on the Browser tab is not even a completely full-featured web browser (it can't even play the animated GIFs on the README.md page). This embedded browser is good enough for downloading pages from www.epguides.com , but will not necessarily show correctly every web URL that you might manually type in.

The App creates some working dBase DBF files, which it uses to lookup standardized names for those files, and then offers to change your filenames into the new standardized formats.
